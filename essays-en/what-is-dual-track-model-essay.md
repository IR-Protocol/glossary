# What Is the Dual-Track Model? — Rent and Electricity for AI

## One-Liner
Dual-Track Model separates AI costs into storage rent (fixed) and compute electricity (usage‑based), ending the unfair subscription bundling that hurts light users, heavy users, and platforms alike.

## Context
Have you ever wondered why you pay for an AI subscription and still get throttled? The answer is simple: subscriptions bundle two fundamentally different costs into one fixed price. Storage — your conversation history, preferences, project files — has a fixed cost, like renting an apartment. Compute — the tokens consumed each time the AI replies — has a variable cost, like your electricity bill. Subscriptions treat rent and electricity as one package. Revenue is fixed, costs float. The math never works. Platforms secretly throttle heavy users, light users feel they waste their monthly fee, and heavy users never get enough.

## Insight
The Dual-Track Model [IR-DT-002] decouples these two accounts. **Storage track — pay rent.** You rent a private cloud memory space monthly. Your chat history, project files, and preferences stay there forever, even when you switch devices or clear cache. The cost is fixed because your occupied storage space is fixed. **Compute track — pay electricity.** Each AI response consumes compute power. You pay per token for what you actually use, nothing more. The cost floats with your usage. After decoupling, the math balances. Light users stop subsidizing others. Heavy users pay for every extra token they consume. Platforms no longer need hidden throttling because costs have corresponding revenue. Rent-plus-electricity is the world’s most stable billing structure — no one bundles apartment rent with electricity bills because it simply cannot work. AI must return to this common sense.

## Implication
Dual-Track is not a pricing tweak; it fundamentally corrects AI accounting. The Memory Chain targets relationship chains; the Dual-Track Model targets rent‑electricity separation. One solves who owns your memory, the other solves how to bill fairly. Together they transform AI from a one‑time tool into a digital companion that stays with you forever.
